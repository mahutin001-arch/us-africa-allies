Folio IX  ·  Programs & Initiatives  ·  Standing Programs of the Foundation

Five Lines of
Sustained Work.

Each program is a standing commitment, not a campaign. The list below is read in order; each entry presumes the one before it.

I.

Inaugural roundtable · Washington · 2026

Program I

Diplomatic Convening

Formal and back-channel gatherings of American and African principals — heads of state, legislators, ministers, ambassadors — convened around named questions of mutual consequence.

Convenings are conducted under the Foundation's standing rules of engagement: prepared papers, agreed agenda, public communiqué where appropriate.

  • CadenceQuarterly
  • SettingsWashington · African capitals · Neutral ground
  • OutputJoint communiqué · Brief
II.

Policy working session · Washington · 2026

Program II

Policy & Research

Long-form briefs, white papers, and joint statements developed with university partners on both continents. Open access. Citable. Read by working governments.

  • CadenceContinuous
  • AuthorsFoundation fellows · Partner faculty
  • ArchiveOpen →
III.

Trade & investment dialogue · 2026

Program III

Economic Bridges

Trade missions, investment forums, and infrastructure dialogues that move capital in both directions. We do not broker transactions; we convene the conditions for them.

  • CadenceQuarterly missions
  • SectorsEnergy · Tech · Agribusiness · Infra
  • OutputSector dossiers · Investment briefs
IV.

Diaspora gathering · 2026

Program IV

Diaspora Council

A standing body of one thousand members across the United States, organized into chapters, with a permanent seat at the Foundation's annual convening. The diaspora as a constitutional bridge — given organizational form.

  • CadenceStanding · Annual plenary
  • FormChapters · Working groups
  • EnrollmentApply →
V.

Inaugural fellowship cohort · 2027 (forthcoming)

Program V

Next-Generation Leadership

Fellowships, scholarships, and exchanges that place rising American and African leaders in deliberate proximity — early, and for long enough that it matters when they are running things.

  • CadenceAnnual cohort · 12-month tenure
  • CompositionHalf American · Half African
