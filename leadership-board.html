The Standing Body
of the Foundation.

The Leadership Board is responsible for the direction, stewardship, and integrity of the U.S.–Africa Allies Foundation. Members are appointed for staggered terms.

§ I   Founder & President
Daniel Edah
Founder · President · Convener
Founder and President of the U.S.–Africa Allies Foundation. Daniel Edah's career has spanned international development, diplomatic convening, and private enterprise across both continents. He chartered the Foundation in 2026 to give the U.S.–Africa partnership the institutional permanence it has long deserved.
