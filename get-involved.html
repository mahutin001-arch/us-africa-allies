Take Your Place
in the Work.

There are several ways to enter the Foundation. Each is a real seat at the table — chosen, recorded, and acknowledged.

§ I   Five Ways to Enter

Pick the seat that fits you.

  1. Founding Member

    For individuals who wish to be part of the Foundation's first cohort of supporters. Annual subscription. Named in the public record. Two convenings per year.

    Subscribe →

  2. Diaspora Council Member

    For members of the African diaspora in the United States who wish to organize their civic and professional weight around the Foundation's work. Open enrollment.

    Enroll →

  3. Fellow

    For early-career American and African leaders. A twelve-month exchange under the Foundation's seal. Inaugural cohort opens 2027. Applications by invitation and open call.

    View Open Calls →

  4. Volunteer · Working Group

    For those who wish to lend professional skill — research, editorial, convening logistics, translation, communications — to the work directly.

    Write Us →

  5. Institutional Partner

    For universities, think tanks, professional associations, and civic organizations whose mandate aligns with the Foundation's. Standing partnerships, named convenings, joint publications.

    Inquire →
