A Movement That Needs You

Transformation Is the Work
of a Community

The U.S.-AFRICA ALLIES FOUNDATION invites you to become part of a movement that uplifts families, empowers youth, strengthens women, and builds ethical leadership across the United States and Africa. Every movement in history was built by individuals who said, "I will make a difference."
Ways to Join

Choose Your Pathway

01
Volunteer

Become a Volunteer

Volunteers are the heartbeat of our movement. Through service, compassion, and dedication, you help deliver programs and support communities.

  • Youth mentorship
  • Women's empowerment support
  • Humanitarian aid distribution
  • Administrative & event coordination
02
Mentor

Serve as a Mentor

Mentors change lives. By guiding youth and women through education and leadership development, mentors shape the next generation of leaders.

  • Academic and career guidance
  • Leadership coaching
  • Life skills development
  • Personal empowerment sessions
03
Committee

Join a Committee

Passionate about governance, programs, or strategy? Contribute your expertise to one of our working committees.

  • Programs Committee
  • Finance & Audit Committee
  • Leadership & Governance Committee
  • Fundraising & Partnerships Committee
04
Ambassador

Program Ambassador

Ambassadors represent the Foundation's mission in their communities — building relationships, supporting events, and mobilizing resources.

  • Community outreach
  • Event support and promotion
  • Resource mobilization
  • Social media advocacy
05
Donor

Support as a Donor

Financial partners allow the Foundation to grow, scale, and respond quickly to community needs across both continents.

  • Scholarships for students
  • Training for emerging leaders
  • Food and support for families
  • Women's empowerment programs
06
Intern

Internships & Pathways

We offer internship opportunities for youth, students, and young professionals who want hands-on experience in community development and leadership.

  • Program support & research
  • Event planning
  • Communications & outreach
  • Leadership development
How to Get Started

Three Simple Steps

Becoming part of the U.S.-AFRICA ALLIES FOUNDATION is simple. Choose a pathway, submit your interest, and our team will connect with you personally.

  • Choose your involvement pathway above
  • Submit your interest via our contact form
  • Our team connects with you within 48 hours
  • Begin your journey with the Foundation
