Your Gift Changes Lives

Give
Today

A donation to the U.S.-Africa Allies Foundation is more than charity — it is an investment in God's work through people.

Why Your Support Matters

Every Contribution Creates
Lasting Impact

Your generosity fuels programs that create lasting, measurable impact across the United States and Africa. When you give, you join a faith-driven movement dedicated to human dignity, opportunity, and long-term transformation.

  • Scholarships for underserved students to pursue education and leadership
  • Leadership development programs for youth and emerging leaders
  • Women's empowerment and entrepreneurship initiatives
  • Humanitarian relief for families facing crisis
  • Community development and outreach programs
  • U.S.–Africa civic, cultural, and faith-based exchange initiatives
Ways to Give

Choose How You Want to Give

One-Time

One-Time Donation

A single contribution that immediately supports programs, outreach, and humanitarian assistance wherever it is needed most.

Monthly

Monthly Partnership

Monthly partners sustain our long-term mission. Even modest recurring gifts create continuous impact, allowing us to expand programs with stability and confidence.

Annual

Annual Giving

Annual donors support large-scale initiatives and provide the foundation with the strength to plan, grow, and develop new leadership and education programs.

Corporate

Corporate & Foundation

We welcome grants, sponsorships, CSR partnerships, and corporate philanthropic support. Organizations may sponsor programs, events, or trainings.

Specific Funds

Sponsor a Specific Cause

Sponsor a Student

Provide direct educational access by covering tuition, books, mentoring, technology, or program participation. Your sponsorship changes a life forever.

Women's Empowerment Fund

Support women-owned businesses, entrepreneurship training, and micro-grant programs. Empower a woman, uplift a family, strengthen a community.

Leadership Academy Fund

Help train the next generation of ethical, faith-driven leaders across schools, churches, civic institutions, and communities.

Humanitarian Relief Fund

Provide direct support to families facing crisis, food insecurity, displacement, or instability. Your gift restores dignity and hope in times of need.

Community Development

Support neighborhood outreach programs, civic engagement, and infrastructure that builds lasting community resilience.

U.S.–Africa Exchange

Fund cross-continental dialogue, cultural exchange programs, and policy forums that strengthen the U.S.–Africa partnership.

How We Use Your Gift

We Steward Every Gift with Faith and Accountability

We pledge full financial transparency and responsible stewardship of every dollar entrusted to us.

  • Program delivery, expansion, and sustainability
  • Scholarships and educational services
  • Women's empowerment and vocational training
  • Youth leadership development
  • Humanitarian aid and crisis response
  • Operational excellence and compliance
Our Commitment to Donors

You Can Trust Us

  • Full financial transparency and annual impact reports
  • Responsible stewardship of every dollar
  • Compliance with U.S. nonprofit standards
  • Ethical and dignified outreach practices
  • Clear program reporting and regular updates

Your Gift Has
Generational Impact

When you give, you plant seeds that outlive you. You invest in leaders who will strengthen families, uplift communities, and shape nations for decades to come.
